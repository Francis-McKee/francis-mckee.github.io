Calculator Midpoint Finder

A TI calculator math tool to solve quadratic equations on your calculator!

Description:

This is a downloadable file for TI- Calculators that will help you find the 2 roots of a quadratic equation in the form ax^2 + bx + c = 0, or the values of x that make the equation true given values for a, b, and c. Just input the a, b, and c variables when prompted, and this tool will automatically calculate the roots of your equation for you!

Download and Install Instructions:

This program should work on any TI-83 calculators and newer, including the TI-83+, TI-84, TI-84+CE, TI-Nspire, and more! In order to download and use this tool on your calculator, you must have a cable to connect your calculator to your PC (most come with the calculator), as well as software on your computer that you use to send files to your calculator. You can download such software here.

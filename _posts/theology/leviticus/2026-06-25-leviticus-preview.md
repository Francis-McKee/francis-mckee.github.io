Leviticus (Preview)

<br>

**Date:** 1446-1406 BC  
**Author:** Moses  
**Audience:** Ancient Israelites

The purpose of the Book of Leviticus is to show the calling of God's people (particularly the Levites) to minister before Him. The book serves as the moral and ethical law for the Israelites, containing civil, sanitary, ceremonial, moral, and religious regulations.

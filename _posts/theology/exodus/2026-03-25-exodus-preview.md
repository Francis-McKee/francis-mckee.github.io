Exodus (Preview)

<br>

**Date:** 1446-1406 BC  
**Author:** Moses  
**Audience:** Ancient Israelites

The Book of Exodus describes the deliverance of the Israelites from Egypt, their journey to Mount Sinai, and the events that occured during their sojourn there. The primary emphasis in Genesis was on the family of Abraham, but the Book of Exodus focuses on the developing nation of Israel.

The main theme of Exodus is redemption. The deliverance of the children of Israel from bondage in Egypt is a type of all redemption, and Moses who led them is a type of Christ.

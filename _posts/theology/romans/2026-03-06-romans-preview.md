Romans (Preview)

<br>

**Date:** 56-57 AD  
**Author:** The Apostle Paul  
**Audience:** Primarily Gentile  

Main Sections of Romans:
* Educate the believers in the basic doctrines related to salvation (Chapters 1-8)
* To help them understand the unbelief of the Jews and how they benefited from it (Chapters 9-11)
* General principles of the Christian life to be aware of and put into practice (Chapters 12-15)

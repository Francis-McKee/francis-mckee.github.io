Zeeoban

Minimalist puzzle game modeled after Sokoban

Description:

Zeeoban is a singleplayer puzzle game made using the PuzzleScript game engine. The goal of the game is to control the player to push the orange boxes on top of the targets.

Controls:

Desktop/ PC Controls: Use WASD or the arrow keys to move. Press U or Z to undo your previous move, R to restart the level, and X to action. Press Esc at any time to go to the Level Select.
